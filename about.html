About Community Skills Hub

Community Skills Hub is a training provider that supports adults who want to improve their digital skills and confidence.

Our mission

Our mission is to make digital learning simple, welcoming and useful for all learners. We believe everyone should have the chance to build skills for everyday life, work and education.

Who our courses are for

  • Adults with low digital confidence
  • Adults returning to education
  • People seeking employment progression
  • Anyone wanting to improve essential digital skills
